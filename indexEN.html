WELCOME! Multiplica Editora

WE ARE HAPPY TO HAVE YOU HERE!


Multiplica Editora emerged from the desire to help other people put their story on paper, this way, many lives are impacted. Our slogan is: "Everyone has a story to HEAL". Our main objective is to help these stories to be multiplied, thus overflowing knowledge, experiences and transforming lives through words. Based in Brazil and Portugal, we attend worldwide.

Multiplica Divisions:


PUBLISHER is where we do all the Editorial/Publishing part of Books, magazines, pamphlets, newspapers, ebooks. We are able to provide any Editorial service.



MULTIPLICA VISUAL is where we develop any creative part that has graphic and visual elements. COVERS, Layout, Illustrations, Visual Identity, Website Creation, Online Portfolios, Online Curriculum, Video Editing and Vignettes.



MULTIPLICA KIDS we give a special attention to LITTLE WRITERS, as we call our Little BIG Authors! We have a project to launch children's books starting at 2 years old.



MULTIPLICA INTERNATIONAL is the place for you who want to launch your books in other countries. We do the entire process, from translation to availability in physical media and Marketplaces in several countries.
