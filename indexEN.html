WELCOME! Multiplica Editora

We are happy to have you here!


Multiplica Editora emerged from the desire to help other people put their story on paper, this way, many lives are impacted. Our slogan is: "Everyone has a story to HEAL". Our main objective is to help these stories to be multiplied, thus overflowing knowledge, experiences and transforming lives through words. Based in Brazil and Portugal, we attend worldwide.

Get to know Multiplica Divisions:


The PUBLISHER is where we do all the Editorial/Publishing part of Books, magazines, pamphlets, newspapers, ebooks. We are able to provide any Editorial service.



The MULTIPLICA VISUAL is where we develop any creative part that has graphic and visual elements. COVERS, Layout, Illustrations, Visual Identity, Website Creation, Online Portfolios, Online Curriculum, Video Editing and Vignettes..



The MULTIPLICA KIDS is the publisher part with special attention to CHILD WRITERS. We have a project to launch children's books from the age of 2.



The MULTIPLICA INTERNATIONAL is the place for you who want to launch your books in other countries. We do the entire process, from translation to availability in physical media and Marketplaces in several countries.
